## Motor specs:

  - resistance / ampere - 25GA-370:
        No load  : 120 Ohm / 0.1A * 6 = 0.6A   - free wheel
        On load  : 30 Ohm  / 0.4A * 6 = 2.4A   - running
        Max      : 4.6 Ohm / 2.6A * 6 = 15.6A  - stall

  - resistance / ampere - JGA25-370 (12V):
        No load : 200 Ohm / 0.06A * 6 = 0.36A   - free wheel
        On load :  48 Ohm / 0.25A * 6 = 1.5A    - running
